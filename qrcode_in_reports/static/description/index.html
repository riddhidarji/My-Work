Print QR Code in Sales, Purchase, Inventory and Invoice

This module uses to show Qr code in sale order reports, Purchase order reports, vendor bills reports,  Warehouse reports and accounting reports. Very useful to make the process faster, Quickly read sale order, purchase order, invoice , bills, delivery order, picking operation no by QR code  

Sales -> Quotation/order

Sales -> PRO-FORMA Invoice

Customer Invoice -> Invoice

Customer Invoice -> Invoice Without Payment

Purchase -> Purchase Order

Purchase -> Request for Quotation

Vendor Bills -> Invoice

Vendor Bills -> Invoices Without Payment 

Inventory -> Delivery Slip
